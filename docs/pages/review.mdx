# Review

Review is a component displays review information with a rating.

## Properties

<Properties>
  <Property name="rating" type="number">
    The rating of the review. (1-5)
  </Property>
  <Property name="title" type="string">
    The title of the review.
  </Property>
  <Property name="html" type="string">
    The content of the review in html.
  </Property>
  <Property name="author" type="string">
    The author of the review.
  </Property>
  <Property name="date" type="string">
    The date of the review. (e.g. Jan 2020)
  </Property>
  <Property name="datetime" type="string">
    The datetime of the review. (e.g. 2020-01-01T00:00:00.000Z)
  </Property>
</Properties>
